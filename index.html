BMI Calculator App

I designed this BMI Calculator App operating motion graphic feature with PS, Ai, and Adobe XD software for the portfolio and practicing designing tools.

The basic idea is that when the user inputs his heignt and weight, the App calculates and shows the user's BMI evaluation with body condition .

I made this graphic design with LEGO characters to make it more attractive for as many people as possible.

KaKao Pay App

There is an App, used by 99% of Korean population, called 'KaKao messenger'. In this messenger, people also can use financial service, called 'KaKao Pay' and I developed another operating processes with graphic design tools.

User can choose sending or receiving function by dragging the icons to middle area. Next, user selects the person to make service, the amount of money and type.
